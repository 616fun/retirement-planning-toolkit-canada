# Disclaimer

This toolkit is provided for **educational and illustrative purposes only**. It is
**not financial, tax, legal, or investment advice**, and nothing it produces should
be relied upon as such.

- The authors are not financial advisors, accountants, or lawyers.
- All sample data (the "Tremblay household") is **fictional**. Any resemblance to a
  real person's finances is coincidental.
- Projections, Monte Carlo simulations, RRSP-meltdown math, and company-health
  signals are simplified models built on assumptions you control. Real outcomes will
  differ. Models can be wrong.
- Tax brackets, contribution limits (RRSP/TFSA/FHSA/RESP), OAS clawback thresholds,
  CPP/OAS amounts, and RRIF minimum factors **change** — many reset annually or
  quarterly. Verify every figure against current **CRA / Service Canada (canada.ca)**
  guidance before acting. See [`docs/CANADA_RULES.md`](docs/CANADA_RULES.md) for the
  dated parameter reference this toolkit ships with.
- This tool models the **federal** system plus the **provincial** figures you supply.
  Provincial rules differ; **Quebec in particular** runs QPP (not CPP), a separate
  provincial return, and different credits. Confirm your province's specifics.
- The company-health module summarizes **public** market data (and US SEC filing data
  where available). It is a monitoring aid, not a buy/sell recommendation.
  Concentration thresholds are configurable rules of thumb, not guarantees.

**Before making real decisions, consult a qualified fee-only financial planner (look
for a CFP®), an accountant (CPA), and/or an estate lawyer.** You are solely
responsible for your own financial decisions.
